MARData-BU tutorials

MARData is a core facility operating in the Hospital del Mar Research Institute, which includes the biostatistics (MSU) and bioinformatics (BU) units. MARData-BU provides computational support to process omics data with a specific focus on translational research. You can find out more about us here, or contact us via mardata-bu@researchmar.net.

This page is a compilation of tutorials created by the MARData-BU team in order to ease the interpretation of bioinformatic analyses, as well as to allow researchers to perform their own analyses without the requirement of having a previous bioinformatic knowledge.
